About

Tic-tac-toe is a simple and popular game that is played by two players on a 3x3 grid. The players take turns marking the empty squares with their symbols, either X or O. The goal is to make a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of three of the same symbol. The player who does this first wins the game. If the grid is filled and no one has made a line of three, the game is a draw. Tic-tac-toe is also known as noughts and crosses, Xs and Os, or X and 0.

Tic-tac-toe is a simple yet fascinating game that has been enjoyed by generations of people around the world. It is a game that can challenge both the mind and the spirit, as well as provide fun and entertainment

GoTicTacToe application is a 'Tic-tac-toe' game written in 'golang', using 'Ebitengine' game engine, to understand cross platform application binding. It is a two player game, where you will play against the automated player.

How to play

In this App Xs will be a Human Player and 0s will be Automated Player. On Launch of the app, a new game will be automatically created, you can also create a new game by clicking 'New Game' button on the top. You can place your Xs on the board by selecting the empty square in the board when 'Your Turn' appears. You can win the game by forming the line of three Xs either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. You can tie the game by obstructing the automated player to form the line of three Os.

Score

The app will maintain you play information (Total Played, Total Win, Total Tied, and Top three Wins). which will be displayed in the score dialog. You can reset these information, which cannot to undone.

Settings

Game Sound - You can 'Mute' or 'Un-Mute' the game sound in settings dialog.

Diplay Timer - You turn timer display 'ON' or 'OFF' in settings dialog.
